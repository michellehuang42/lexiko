HERE'S HOW IT WORKS

1. Upload a lab report

With 3 clicks, securely upload your health reports online for a free analysis.

DEMO

2. Answer a few simple questions

While your results process, fill out a brief 5-minute questionnaire to refine your health data for more optimal results and recommendations.

3. Create a personalized plan

Interact with your data to create a customized plan to a healthier life: set goals and notifications to track your progress along the way.

If you like what you’re seeing, create an account for more functionalities:

  •  Monthly diagnostic health reports
  •  Communication platform with doctors in your network
  •  Access to network of hospitals and clinics we’ve partnered with
  •  Hassle-free transfer of your medical documents between institutions

...and more!

Ready to test it out?

Even if you’re unsure, you can try a demo first with a sample health report to see what the results would look like, or feel free to contact us if you have any questions — we’re here to help!
